self-study
strategies

Successful study does not depend on how much one studies, but rather how well one does it. Students often have recourse to practices that, besides making them study for longer hours, are counterproductive in nature. This workshop offers them the necessary tools and best practices for learning in an effective manner, not only reducing time spent on it, but also achieving better results.

They will learn how to:
  • Examine personal learning methods critically and address weaknesses
  • Emphasise the importance of understanding over cramming
  • Analyse extensive texts with ease and confidence through active reading
  • Understand the purpose of highlighting texts and learn the effective approach
  • Make efficient use of personal summaries without compromising the content
  • Apply tried-and-true memory techniques that facilitate retention and later revision
  • Adopt a step-by-step reliable study method combining all skills learned
Skills developed:
  • Reading Comprehension Skills
  • Highlighting Skills
  • Summary Writing Skills
  • Memory Skills

STRATEGIES
